What is Mood Meter ?

  • Research conducted at CHUS Fleurimont in Sherbrooke, Quebec.
  • Developed the application specifically for the research.
  • Languages JS/HTML/CSS

Mood Meter is a simple yet impactful application that was developed for pediatric doctors Dorothée Roch and Sylvie Lafrenaye as part of a residency project at CHUS Fleurimont hospital in Sherbrooke, Quebec. The application was designed to support research focused on children with chronic pain and their ability to express emotions.

The core functionality of Mood Meter involves presenting children with a series of questions and scenarios, where they are encouraged to express their emotions by clicking on a scale rather than verbalizing their feelings. This approach aims to provide a more accurate and comfortable way for children to communicate their emotions, potentially leading to better understanding and management of their chronic pain.
